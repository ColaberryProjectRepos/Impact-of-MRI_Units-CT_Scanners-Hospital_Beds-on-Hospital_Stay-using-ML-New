Problem Statement:
Imagine a healthcare system where hospitals struggle to manage patient flow due to inefficient resource allocation. According to recent studies, a staggering 25% of hospitals face significant challenges in optimizing their resources effectively, leading to increased patient wait times and decreased overall quality of care. This project addresses this critical issue by harnessing the power of data science to predict and enhance hospital efficiency, ensuring timely and optimal use of resources.
